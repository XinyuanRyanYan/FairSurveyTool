Consent Cover Letter

The purpose of this research study is to understand how human process and understand different form and formats of information presentation be it visual or textual. As part of the survey, you will be presented with information about algorithms and how they work to provide predictions. Based on the content and format of information, you will be asked to respond to questions that will evaluate how well you understood the information and how you perceived the information environment.

All information will be kept confidential by the researcher. The researcher will not identify the study questionnaires or study data with your name. The data files will be on computers that are password protected. If a report or article is written about this study then the study results will be described in a summarized manner so that you cannot be identified. Any data that is published or made public will be anonymized so that you cannot be identified.

If you have any questions complaints or if you feel you have been harmed by this research please contact Bei Wang Phillips, University of Utah Business School, beiwang@sci.utah.edu or xyan@cs.utah.edu.

Contact the Institutional Review Board (IRB) if you have questions regarding your rights as a research participant. Also, contact the IRB if you have questions, complaints or concerns which you do not feel you can discuss with the investigator. The University of Utah IRB may be reached by phone at (801) 581-3655 or by e-mail at irb@hsc.utah.edu.

Research Participant Advocate: You may also contact the Research Participant Advocate (RPA) by phone at (801) 581-3803 or by email at participant.advocate@hsc.utah.edu.

Participation in this study is voluntary. You can choose not to take part. You can choose not to finish the survey. We check responses carefully in order to make sure that people have read the instructions for the task and responded carefully. We will only accept participants who clearly demonstrate that they have read and understood the survey. There may be some very simple questions in what follows that test whether you are reading the instructions. If you get these questions wrong, you will not be eligible for participation.

By clicking “continue” you are giving your consent to participate.

We appreciate your participation in the study. Thank you.
