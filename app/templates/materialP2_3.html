Disparate Impact (DI) compares the proportion of individuals in the unprivileged group who received a favorable prediction to the proportion of individuals in the privileged group who received a favorable prediction. As highlighted in the above visualization, the calculation involves the proportion of the unprivileged group that received the positive outcome divided by the proportion of the privileged group that received the positive outcome. The ideal value of this metric is $1.0$ or $100\%$, and the value in this example would be $(60/100)/(70/100)= 0.85$ or $85\%$.
